Our Evolution

The Journey of Eco Voyage

2018

The Awakening

It started in the dense trails of Thrissur. A group of passionate volunteers saw the destruction caused by mass tourism. We realized that if we didn't step up to protect the Western Ghats, no one would.

2022

Empowering Tribes

We expanded our vision by partnering directly with indigenous tribes. We built sustainable eco-camps where the revenue went straight into the hands of the locals, turning them into forest guardians.

2026

A Global Standard

Today, we proudly lead the carbon-neutral travel movement. From planting thousands of trees to saving tons of plastic, Eco Voyage proves that exploring the world can actually help heal it.

Our Mission
